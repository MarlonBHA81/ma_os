# Brand Messaging Playbook

The core deliverable of the [[Brand Messaging Session]] — the anchor message assets every other asset is built from.

## The three core assets

1. **Brand Message** — the central narrative / what the brand stands for and says.
2. **One-liner** — the single-sentence statement of what you do and why it matters (the clarity test the [[BRS Scanner]] measures).
3. **Tagline** — the short, memorable line.

These three drive all [[Brand Messaging Session|channel copy]] and the [[Wireframe page set]]. If they're sharp, everything downstream is easier; if they're vague, the whole build inherits the fuzziness.
