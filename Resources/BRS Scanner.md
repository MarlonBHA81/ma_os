# BRS Scanner

The Brand Resonance Scanner — the automated diagnostic at the top of [[Story Advantage Marketing Agency]]'s funnel. A prospect answers strategic questions, gets a score + result category, and is routed into the matching email nurture band and review offer. It's the entry point of the [[Comprehensive Marketing Review]] ladder and scores against the [[Brand Resonance System]].

There are two related scoring instruments in the master doc:

## Website assessment — 5 strategic questions

Each scored 1–4. **Reverse scoring: lower = healthier**, higher = more problems (creates urgency). All five equally weighted, one per system pillar.

1. **Website Clarity Test** — can visitors grasp what you do & why to choose you? (grunt test → *Differentiate*)
2. **Brand Consistency Audit** — is brand voice consistent across channels? (→ *Integrate*)
3. **Authority Translation Gap** — online presence vs offline reputation. **THE qualifying question** (→ core problem)
4. **Results Reality Check** — online marketing vs referral growth (→ *Activate*)
5. **Pain Point Prioritisation** — biggest frustration → buying motivation

### 4 result categories (total 5–20 pts)

- **Champion (5–8)** — strong online, minimal gaps → "Scale your success"
- **Builder (9–12)** — good foundation, some gaps → "Close the offline/online gap"
- **Opportunity (13–16)** — significant disconnect → "Transform your digital presence"
- **Foundation / Rebuild (17–20)** — complete overhaul → "Start your transformation"

Most prospects land 9–16 (Builder/Opportunity) — the qualified buying zone.

## Quiz variant — 6 questions, 4 bands

A separate 6-question "Brand Resonance Quiz" scores 0–24 into: **Invisible (0–7), Emerging (8–13), Competing (14–19), Resonating (20–24)** — each with its own 5-email Quick Wins course.
