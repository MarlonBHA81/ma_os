# Design stage

Stage 4 of the [[Story Advantage delivery process]]. The [[Wireframe page set]] gets its visual treatment.

## What goes in
- **Brand identity + colours** applied to the design.
- Visual direction **chosen from a library of ~1000s of design options** (a mix to pick from), matched to the brand.

Turns the structural wireframes into a designed look ready for [[Development tracks|build]].
