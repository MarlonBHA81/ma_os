# MMR Template

The **Mini Marketing Review** — tier 1 (entry / light) of the [[Comprehensive Marketing Review|review ladder]]. Fast, largely driven by the [[BRS Scanner]] result. Purpose: give a prospect a real, useful snapshot in minutes and qualify them toward the [[CMR Template|CMR]].

## Inputs
- A [[BRS Scanner]] session (the prospect's answers + score + result category).
- A quick look at the live site (grunt test, top-of-page only).

## Structure (keep it to ~1 page)

1. **Score & category** — the scanner total and its band (Champion / Builder / Opportunity / Foundation), stated plainly.
2. **What your score means** — 2–3 sentences using the category's messaging, tied to the [[Brand Resonance System]] phase most at risk (Differentiate / Integrate / Activate).
3. **Top 3 quick wins** — the highest Impact / lowest Effort items only (a subset of what a full [[CMR Template|CMR]] would surface). Concrete and doable this week.
4. **One clear next step** — the CTA up the ladder: book a [[CMR Template|Comprehensive Marketing Review]].

## Principle
The MMR must be genuinely valuable on its own — reverse scoring already created the urgency; the MMR shows you can help. Don't dump the whole CMR here; leave the depth as the reason to upgrade.
