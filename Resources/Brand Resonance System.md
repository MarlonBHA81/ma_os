# Brand Resonance System

Story Advantage's proprietary methodology (Brand Resonance System™) for transforming established businesses with strong **offline** reputations into businesses with matching **online** authority and revenue. Tagline logic: *Resonance = Results = Revenue*.

Used by [[Story Advantage Marketing Agency]]. It's the framework every review and the [[BRS Scanner]] score maps back to.

## The 3 phases

1. **DIFFERENTIATE** — brand story foundation, market authority positioning, audience segmentation. (Solves an unclear value proposition.)
2. **INTEGRATE** — unified brand voice across all channels; omnichannel deployment; customer value journey. (Solves inconsistent, fragmented marketing.)
3. **ACTIVATE** — lead-generation and conversion systems, authority-building content, paid growth. (Solves referral-dependence / "online doesn't convert".)

## The core problem it targets

The **Authority Translation Gap**: companies whose offline reputation is far stronger than their online presence. Closing that gap is the qualifying wedge for the whole system — see [[BRS Scanner]] Question 3.

## Delivery vehicles

- [[BRS Scanner]] — the diagnostic quiz/scan at the top of the funnel.
- [[Comprehensive Marketing Review]] — the MMR / CMR / SMS review ladder.
- Also expressed as a 180-day transformation guide, a 5-part video blueprint, and a live signature process.
