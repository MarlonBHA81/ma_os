# SMS Template

The **Strategic Marketing Session** — tier 3 (top) of the [[Comprehensive Marketing Review|review ladder]]. A live strategy session that turns a completed [[CMR Template|CMR]]'s gaps into a prioritised growth plan. Derived from the worked example [[Strategy — SAMA revenue doubling]].

## Input
A finished [[CMR Template|CMR]] (scores + prioritised gaps) for the client's site.

## Structure

1. **Executive Summary** — the thesis: which 2–3 integrated campaigns get them to the target (e.g. 2x revenue in 6–12 months), and the single Priority 1 move.
2. **Current Situation** — Strengths / Critical Gaps (blocking growth) / Market Context. Pulls directly from the CMR.
3. **Top 3 Campaigns** — for each: one-line goal, core idea & positioning, primary ICP, mechanics, and how it maps to a [[Brand Resonance System]] phase.
4. **Prioritisation & Roadmap** — rank campaigns by Impact × Feasibility; a 90-day implementation roadmap.
5. **Assumptions, Kill Criteria & Review Cadence** — what must be true, the red lines that trigger a pivot, and the review rhythm (this is what makes it a *system*, not a one-off).
6. **Financial Projections** — the revenue model toward the target.
7. **Strategic Thesis** — the one-paragraph "why this wins."

## Where it sits
Scanner → [[MMR Template|MMR]] → [[CMR Template|CMR]] → **SMS**. The SMS is where diagnosis becomes a paid engagement / delivery plan.
