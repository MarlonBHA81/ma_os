# 🏠 Home

The front door to my brain. Everything is organized **by purpose**, not by tag.

## Areas of the brain

- [[Projects]] — active, goal-driven work with a finish line
- [[Areas]] — ongoing responsibilities I maintain over time
- [[Resources]] — reference material and topics I care about
- [[Archive]] — things that are done or dormant
- [[Daily]] — the running capture log (raw thoughts land here first)
- [[Maps]] — hub notes that gather related ideas across folders

## How this works

New information gets written as a small, self-contained note and **wikilinked** to whatever it relates to. The links are what make this a brain instead of a folder of files. See [[How ma_os works]] for the operating rules.
