# Comprehensive Marketing Review

The map of [[Story Advantage Marketing Agency]]'s marketing/website review process — a 3-part ladder that turns a [[BRS Scanner]] result into a paid engagement. Everything scores against the [[Brand Resonance System]].

> Naming note: the labels **MMR / CMR / SMS are Marlon's productised packaging** and do **not** appear in the master strategy doc yet. The doc supplies the *instruments* (scanner/quiz, the 5-question website assessment); the packaging sits on top.

## The three parts

| Part | Name | What it is | Status of the template |
|------|------|-----------|------------------------|
| 1 | **MMR** — Mini Marketing Review | Light / entry review. Likely the [[BRS Scanner]] score + top quick wins. | To define |
| 2 | **CMR** — Comprehensive Marketing Review | Full deep-dive website + marketing assessment, scored /100 with prioritised opportunities. | **Worked example exists** — storyadvantage.agency, 62/100 (2026-02-28) in the old vault |
| 3 | **SMS** — Strategic Marketing Session | Live strategy session translating the review into a plan. | To define |

## What a CMR already looks like (worked example)

The 62/100 review in the old `Obsidian Vault` assessed: Analytics & Tracking (4/10), Social Distribution (38/100), plus SEO/messaging/etc., producing P0–P2 opportunities and quick wins. That doc is effectively the **CMR deliverable template** to formalise.
