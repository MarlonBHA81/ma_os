# Comprehensive Marketing Review

The map of [[Story Advantage Marketing Agency]]'s marketing/website review process — a 3-part ladder that turns a [[BRS Scanner]] result into a paid engagement. Everything scores against the [[Brand Resonance System]].

> Naming note: the labels **MMR / CMR / SMS are Marlon's productised packaging** and do **not** appear in the master strategy doc yet. The doc supplies the *instruments* (scanner/quiz, the 5-question website assessment); the packaging sits on top.

## The ladder (all three tiers now defined)

Scanner → **MMR** → **CMR** → **SMS** — each tier goes deeper and moves the prospect up.

| Tier | Name | What it is | Template | Worked example |
|------|------|-----------|----------|----------------|
| 0 | [[BRS Scanner]] | Automated diagnostic quiz/scan → score + category | (instrument) | — |
| 1 | **MMR** — Mini Marketing Review | Fast, scanner-driven snapshot + top 3 quick wins | [[MMR Template]] | — |
| 2 | **CMR** — Comprehensive Marketing Review | Full 8-framework website assessment scored /100 | [[CMR Template]] | [[Review — SAMA website 62-100]] |
| 3 | **SMS** — Strategic Marketing Session | Live session turning gaps into a campaign roadmap | [[SMS Template]] | [[Strategy — SAMA revenue doubling]] |

Everything scores against the [[Brand Resonance System]] phases (Differentiate / Integrate / Activate). This review is **stage 1** of the [[Story Advantage delivery process]].

## Feeding it with real data (the improvement loop)

- **Input:** a real [[BRS Scanner]] session (prospect answers + score).
- **Where sessions live:** **not Cloudflare** (both accounts are empty) — the app is most likely on **GoHighLevel** (the master doc has "GHL Implementation Notes") with the site on WordPress/Divi.
- **Open decision:** how to pipe live sessions in — GHL API (best for an ongoing loop), a GHL→Google Sheet automation, or manual CSV export.
- **The loop Marlon wants:** live sessions in → analyse patterns across sessions → sharpen the MMR/CMR/SMS output over time. Blocked only on the data pipe above.
